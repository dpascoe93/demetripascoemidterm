about

The name's Demetri Pascoe. Copywriter by trade, self-proclaimed fashion icon by choice. I'm currently finishing up my last semester at Temple University after spending my summer in Chicago as a Copywriting Intern at DigitasLBi. At Digitas, I wrote for Sprint and Whirlpool, while also managing to get in on some pitch work for Glidden Paint. It's also where I got my start as a lower-torso/upper-thigh model while helping out on some social content for Miller Lite.

Occasionally you'll find me biking around the City of Brotherly Love, making every Taco Tuesday deal a personal challenge, or hosting a late-night style talk show. But usually you'll catch me writing. Ads, scripts, Yelp reviews, or even Tinder bios. Especially Tinder bios. Give me shout if you want to talk about my online shopping addiction or job opportunities; preferably the latter.

work

#goforlast.

In a world where everyone wants to be first, we wanted to flip the script. Those who come last aren't neccessarily the losers, and sometimes it's the last that decide who's first. It's not the first rep that makes you stronger; it's that last rep. The one you must power through. This is the last we strive for. The #GoForLast campaign highlights reasons why you should always strive for last.

is it even a question?

Snapple is the fun, quirky drink in the oversaturated RTD beverage category. As a brand that offers such an unique experience, choosing Snapple should be a no-brainer. This campaign answers the question of "What to drink?' by positioning Snapple as the obvious choice. As the client for the 2016 National Student Advertising Competiton, we were tasked with creating a fully integrated marketing campaign with a dual strategy focusing on two different target audiences. For this project I was one of the Lead Copywriters, Editors and Presenters. Our team received Honorable Mention in the District 11 Competition for our pitch.
